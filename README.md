# 写作的标注 (Writing Annotations)

Manage footnotes, draft variants, and annotations seamlessly. Perfect for novelists and creative writers.

这是一个专为小说创作者、编辑和创意写作者设计的 Obsidian 插件。它提供了强大的脚注和大纲变体标注管理功能，支持同一文本的多种拟稿方案对比与一键切换。

## Key Features (核心功能)

- **Footnote Compass (脚注导航)**: Easily browse and navigate through all footnotes in your active manuscript.
- **Draft Variants (变体草稿切换)**: Support multiple drafts/variants for a single phrase or sentence, with one-click toggles and preview.
- **Exporting Drafts (变体全文导出)**: Compile and export your final version of the manuscript based on your checked variants.
- **Auto-Healing Search (自愈搜索)**: Securely track annotations even if you modify nearby text.

## How to Use (如何使用)

1. Select any text in your editor, right-click, and choose **"添加正文变体标注"** (Add text variant annotation).
2. Open the sidebar panel by clicking the ribbon icon.
3. Manage, edit, and toggle between different variants or navigate through footnotes in the panel.
